OCR and Computer Vision Project

Welcome to our real-time notification system powered by OCR and computer vision technology.

How It Works

Our system utilizes cameras to capture images of vehicle number plates. These images are processed using OCR to extract the number plate information. Then, computer vision algorithms determine the location and timestamp of the vehicle.

Real-time Notifications

Once the vehicle's information is processed, we send instant notifications to the respective owners. No matter where their vehicles are spotted, they'll receive timely updates about the location and time.
